Data & Privacy

Account & Data
Deletion Request

You have the right to delete your Parauxo account and the personal data associated with it. This page explains exactly how to do that and what happens to your data.

How to request

Steps to Delete Your Account

1
Compose a deletion request email

Open your email client and create a new message addressed to support@parallaxhorizon.com.

2
Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

This helps our team identify and prioritise your request immediately.

3
Include your account details in the body

Please provide the name and email address associated with your Parauxo account so we can locate it accurately.

4
Wait for confirmation

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been permanently deleted.

What happens to your data

Data Deleted & Retained

When your deletion request is processed, here is exactly what is removed and what, if anything, is kept and why.

Data Type Action Details & Retention Period
Name Deleted Permanently removed from all active systems within 30 days of your confirmed request.
Email address Deleted Permanently removed from all active systems within 30 days of your confirmed request.
Account & profile data Deleted All account settings and preferences are permanently erased within 30 days.
Anonymised usage logs Retained up to 90 days Aggregate, anonymised analytics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for up to 90 days for product improvement, then deleted.
Legal & financial records Retained per legal obligation Where required by applicable law (e.g. tax records), certain transactional data may be retained for up to 7 years before deletion.
⚠️ Once your personal data is deleted, this action cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any content you wish to keep before submitting your request.
