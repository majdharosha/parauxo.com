Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2025  ·  Last Updated: February 19, 2025

Parauxo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how you can request deletion, and your rights — in full compliance with Google Play's requirements and applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.

1 Information We Collect

The table below summarizes all data types collected by the App, aligned with Google Play's Data Safety categories:

Data Type Examples Collected Shared with Third Parties
Personal identifiers Name, email address Yes No
Content you submit Captions, hooks, post ideas Yes No
App activity Features used, analyses run, session data Yes No
Financial info Subscription billing (processed by Stripe) Yes Processor only
Device / technical info Device type, OS version, IP address Yes No
Location data Precise or approximate location No No
Photos / media / files Camera, microphone, gallery No No
Contacts Address book No No
Messages / communications Emails, SMS, in-app messages No No

We do not collect social media credentials, follower counts, or any data from third-party social platforms.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver AI-powered content feedback and improvement suggestions
  • Enforce your plan's monthly usage limits
  • Process subscription payments securely via Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, account notifications)
  • Improve App performance using anonymized, aggregated data only
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your submitted content to train AI models without your explicit consent. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data.

3 AI Content Processing

When you submit content (captions, hooks, ideas) for analysis:

  • It is processed in real time to generate your feedback results.
  • It is not permanently stored unless you explicitly choose to save it.
  • Unsaved submissions are deleted at the end of your session.
  • It is never shared with other users or used for advertising.
  • Third-party AI providers we may use are bound by data processing agreements that prohibit them from using your content for their own training or commercial purposes.

4 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly as necessary:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Your card details are handled directly by Stripe and never stored by us.
  • AI infrastructure providers — to process content analyses, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Cloud hosting providers — for secure App infrastructure and storage.
  • Analytics providers — aggregated, anonymized usage data only (no personal identifiers).
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

5 Data Retention & Deletion

  • Unsaved analyses — deleted immediately at session end.
  • Saved analyses — retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Account data — deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • Billing records — retained up to 7 years as required by financial regulations.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, use the "Delete Account" option in App Settings, or email privacy@parauxo.com. We will complete the deletion within 30 days.

6 Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all data transfers
  • Encryption at rest for stored data
  • Strict access controls — only authorized personnel may access user data
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt out — opt out of any non-essential data processing

To exercise any right, email privacy@parauxo.com with the subject line "Privacy Request – [Your Name]". We will respond within 30 days.

8 Children's Privacy

Parauxo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@parauxo.com and we will delete it promptly.

9 International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your own, including the United States. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) to ensure your data is protected to an adequate standard.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. For material changes, we will notify you via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of the App after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or deletion inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@parauxo.com
  • Subject line: Privacy Request – [Your Name]

We respond to all inquiries within 30 days.
